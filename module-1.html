Module 01

Student Wellbeing

Why are so many students experiencing poor mental health? This module examines the evidence on psychological distress in Australian universities, introduces frameworks for understanding what shapes student wellbeing, and gives educators practical strategies to act on what they learn.

~75 min 6 lessons Includes video Quiz included
What shapes student wellbeing at university
Rates and patterns of psychological distress
What educators are positioned to change

Module contents

Work through the lessons in order, or jump to a topic. Each lesson takes 10–15 minutes.

  1. 1
    Introduction Overview of the module and what you’ll learn
  2. 2
    Mental Health in Higher Education Rates and patterns of psychological distress among university students in Australia and internationally
  3. 3
    Student Life in the 21st Century How contemporary pressures (financial stress, employment demands, social media) shape wellbeing
  4. 4
    Wellbeing Essentials The core components of psychological wellbeing and the evidence base behind them
  5. 5
    Fostering Student Wellbeing Practical strategies educators and institutions can use to support student mental health in learning environments
  6. ✓
    Knowledge Check Test your understanding of Module 1 content
