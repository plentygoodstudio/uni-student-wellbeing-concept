The five modules

Each module stands on its own, but they’re designed to build on each other. Most educators start with Module 1.

Module 01

Student Wellbeing

Why are so many students experiencing poor mental health? Experts answer questions about the high rates of student distress and the university’s role in promoting wellbeing. The essential foundation for the rest of this program.

How it works

1

Pick a module

Start with Module 1 if you’re new, or jump to whichever topic is most relevant to your current work.

2

Work through the lessons

Each module has 5–6 lessons including video, written content, case studies, and a knowledge check. Work at your own pace.

3

Apply to your teaching

Each module ends with practical strategies designed to be applied to real curriculum and teaching contexts. Not just theory.

Who it’s for

These modules were designed for anyone who teaches or supports students at university.

  • Lecturers and senior lecturers
  • Tutors and demonstrators
  • Course coordinators
  • PhD supervisors
  • Professional staff in student support roles
  • Academic developers
  • Faculty leadership
Ready to start?

Begin with the foundation, or jump in anywhere.

Each module takes about an hour. Module 1 sets up the rest of the program, but you can start with whichever topic matches your teaching context.
